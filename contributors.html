Contributors

The original developers of the Wilde Trials International News Archive are Colette Colligan, Michael Joyce, Sarah Bull, and Cécile Loyen. Development of the database and its applications began in 2014 and has continued collaboratively since.

The archive is affiliated with SFU’s Digital Humanities Innovation Lab and Transfopress, and operates out of Simon Fraser University Library.
